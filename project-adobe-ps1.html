MicroWorld

This is a MicroWorld design created using Adobe Photoshop. The concept presents a miniature, winter-themed world, showcasing detailed elements like igloos, a polar bear, and snow-covered trees, encapsulated within a small cube. The scene is enhanced with a touch of magic, featuring Santa's sleigh flying overhead, adding a whimsical atmosphere.
