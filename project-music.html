FungPeng FungJai

FungPeng FungJai is a website developed using HTML and CSS to create a platform for music recommendations. The website includes a homepage that explains the concept behind its development, a section for trending artists featuring the top 10 trending songs, and a page dedicated to various playlists, allowing listeners to enjoy a wide range of music.

Here are some features of the system:

  • Users can click on the links to listen to music.
  • The website collects and displays top-ranked songs.
  • It offers a variety of playlists.
