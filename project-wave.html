Wave

CWave is designed to meet the needs of music enthusiasts who are passionate about sound and melody. The app features a variety of user-friendly functions, such as recommended tracks, trending artists, playlists, and top charts, offering a great music experience for relaxation and enjoyment. We utilize Dart in conjunction with the Flutter framework for seamless app development.

Here are some features of the system:

  • The application allows users to play music and access information about various artists.
  • It features a function for creating playlists.
  • It includes a feature for ranking top-charting songs.
